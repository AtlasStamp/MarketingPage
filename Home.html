Use spline stamps to make roads, rivers, canyons, shorelines, whatever you can think of splines supports height , color , splatmap control just like the regular stamp objects supports open and closed splines:

add or remove nodes via click and shift + click-
move nodes simply by dragging them-
scale nodes by holding down ctrl and dragging-
subdivide or unsubdivide your spline-
    smooth your spline height wise and/or latheral wise-
