next gen terrain editor for unity

Hi and welcome to Atlas.

We all know how much Unity is running behind on its terrain and how challenging it is to create vast, good looking worlds.

We strongly believe that the future of terrain editing lies in a non destructive object based workflow. As unity veterans we have been working on a next gen terrain editor that combines the brushed and generative workflows into the perfect hybrid. Having both these workflows operating inside a non destructive object based system allows us to overcome the shortcomings that these techniques have on their own.

Where a purely generative technique can be tedious to make specific smaller changes, a purely brushed technique just sounds like slavery. On top of that not having objects requires either a node based editor or some other stackable list that rapidly increases complexity and is challenging to maintain.

To make Atlas accessible to all Unity users we built it as if it comes from Unity developers themselves and kept its flow as close to default Unity as possible. We believe that this is a tool that many of us are looking for and that it can fill the lack of proper terrain support Unity is currently struggling with.

In coming days and weeks we will be sharing more information on this site and our Unity thread. If you have any questions feel free to ask them there.
